AiM Concept

The current healthcare system has a fundamental need: the prescription of physical exercise as a preventive measure, a treatment for diseases, and a way to improve quality of life. This should be supervised by a qualified professional in the field.

If physical exercise were a pill, it would be the most prescribed by doctors, but it seems that there's little interest in it.

This is where AiM is born, a concept designed to fill the gaps in today's healthcare system.

AiM - Personal & Learning Training is a concept that not only trains but also educates on physical exercise, nutrition, and habit improvement tailored to each individual’s context.

It is based on the 7 principles of training applied to the 7 basic movement patterns.

All training is personalized according to the foundation of this formula.

The other part is education: Here, you learn about calorie expenditure, fat loss, strength, habit improvement, injury, nutrition, anatomy, physiology, posture, and more.

Understanding the 'why' behind things is key to achieving adherence to a sustainable lifestyle and plan.

This is the only formula to achieve your goals, but no one tells you that.
